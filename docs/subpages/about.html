About

Hoa Van Thanh is a Graphic Designer living and working in Leipzig.

He was born and raised in Munich and in 2016 he graduated from the Designschule München as a Staatlich geprüfter Kommunikationsdesigner.

In the following month, he moved to Leipzig to study Philosophy at the Universität Leipzig.

Now he works as a freelancing Graphic Designer, moving between the different media, web, print and video.

When he is not working on some project, you will find him somewhere skateboarding, doing Ashtanga Yoga or juggling.
