About me

Hello fellow human beings! I'm a Graphic Designer and Front-End Developer living and working in Leipzig.

I was born and raised in Munich and in 2016 I graduated from the Designschule München as a Staatlich geprüfter Kommunikationsdesigner.

In the following month, I moved to Leipzig to study Philosophy at the Universität Leipzig.

By the end of 2019 I changed my plans once again and started to transition to web, learning the fundamentals of Web Programming and UI/UX Design.

I was immediatley intrigued by the creativity and complexity of web technology and the challenging task of problem solving.

My main object in web developement is to bring together pleasing aesthetic looks and functionality.

I have experience with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Github, and npm. Currently, I am learning Sass and PHP.

Outside of working life, I am passionate about skateboarding, an avid Ashtanga Yoga practitioner, an occasional DJ and juggler, and caffeine addict.

If you have any questions, suggestions or if you want to work with me, go right ahead, contact me!
